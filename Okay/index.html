ABOUT:

The Blobchain Initiative is an endeavor to allow the everyday person to track their progress towards whatever health goals they may have in an easy way. Our desire is to see people inspired by our app to increase their activity levels and better monitor their health habits. We find that when you're shown your own statistics, you're automatically driven to make them better (like in a videogame).

So for all of our users: Get out there, keep going, and stay strong!

TEAM:

This team consists of 4 amazingly smart students currently working towards their bachelor's degrees in Computer Science. They came together through being roommates, but more importantly through having the same awesome programming teacher, Dr. Dean Bushey (hence the group name). They came into this with minimal knowledge of web design, and no knowledge of smart contracts or decentralized apps, and now here they are.
